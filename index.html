    About UCCI

    Udaipur Chamber Of Commerce & Industry

    Founded on 12 th February 1965, UCCI is the multi district apex body of trade, industry, mining, tourism & education of Southern Rajasthan. We have partnered with the industry and government through advisory and consultative processes to bring about socio- economic development in the region.

    UCCI is Southern Rajasthan's premier business association, with a growing membership. At present we have a member base of 569 organisations from diverse sectors. All major local associations are also affiliated with us. Through them we represent about 3000 industrial units across the 8 districts of South Rajasthan including Udaipur, Rajsamand, Chittorgarh, Bhilwara, Banswara, Dungarpur, Pratapgarh and Sirohi which constitute about Rs. 57,000 Crore of investment. We have another 1260+ members across Rajasthan who make use of Rajasthan Waste Management Project.
